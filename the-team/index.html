Meet the Team

Birmingham City University's IoT Initiative is made up of a diverse team of dedicated staff members and students.

  • Adel Aneiba

    Senior Lecturer in Mobile Computing

    Dr. Adel Aneiba is the Initiator and Founder of the IoT Initiative at Birmingham City University.

  • Brett Nangle

    Placement Student

    Brett Nangle is a current placement student at BCU. He is responsible for building the backend for our private LoRaWAN network.

  • Bruno Junior

    Lab Technician

    Bruno Junior is the lab technician for the Innovation Lab, and is responsible for maintaining a collection of equipment and tools for use in building projects.

  • Dalia El-Banna

    Assistant Lecturer in Software Defined Networks

    Dalia El-Banna is the project leader for Connected Birmingham. She personally maintains one LoRa Gateway in the Birmingham area, and contributes her experiences to our gateway configuration wiki.

  • Luke Tainton

    Backend Systems Engineer

    Luke Tainton is a sandwich year Computer Networks student, currently on placement at Cisco. He is responsible for managing community systems such as the GitHub and Slack organisations, as well as designing and maintaining the website.
