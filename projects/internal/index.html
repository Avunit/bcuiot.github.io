Internal Projects

These projects are internal projects to BCU, and will directly influence decisions made at the University. While they may be assisted by external partners, they are not intended for use outside of BCU.


Smart Circulation
Project Aim

The Smart Circulation project was an assessed innovation task as part of Birmingham City University's Innovation Fest. It aims to provide Birmingham City University with information regarding footfall and building utilisation in its Millennium Point facility.

Awards

Smart Circulation won the award for Inspirational Innovation Project.
